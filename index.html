PROGRAM OFFERED

MBA

A two-year MBA program that fosters students with a multidimensional skill set for today's global challenges. The program is spread over 6 trimesters, leading to the degree in Master of Business Administration.

Bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized University with a minimum of 50% marks in the aggregate (all the subjects for which the candidate has taken & appeared) plus two-years of industry experience is desirable.

Program Highlights

  • Established strong corporate credibility over the years
  • Case-based learning with a student-centered approach
  • Facilitates synergy between industry and academia

MBA HR

A two-year MBA HR program equips students to navigate dynamic HR challenges in the corporate world. The program is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of human resource management practices.

Eligibility: Bachelor's degree in any discipline with a minimum of 50% marks in aggregate from a recognized University. Industry experience is a plus.

Program Highlights

  • Focuses on developing HR leadership
  • Case-based and hands-on learning experience
  • Industry-oriented curriculum with internships

MBA Pharmaceutical Management

A specialized two-year MBA program focused on preparing students for the pharmaceutical industry.

Bachelor's or Master's degree in Pharmacy (B.Pharm & M.pharm), BDS, BHMS, BAMS, Biochemistry, Biotechnology, MBBS, Microbiology from a recognized University with a minimum of 50% marks in the aggregate (all the subjects for which the candidate has taken & appeared). Candidates working in Pharmaceutical Companies or with work experience in the healthcare sector can also apply subject to meeting the above qualification criteria.

Program Highlights

  • Ranked as number one program in the country
  • Immerse in rigorous summer internships
  • Engage in 180 Hours of fieldwork and over 100 hours of data analytics

MBA Business Analytics

A comprehensive two-year MBA program emphasizing on Business Analytics for strategic decision-making.

For the MBA - Business Analytics, Mumbai, Bachelor's Degree (10+2+3 or more years of graduation) in Engineering, Mathematics, Statistics, Commerce, Economics, Management Studies, or Computer Science with a minimum of 50% marks in aggregate (all the subjects for which the candidate has taken & appeared) ) plus two-years of industry experience is desirable. Mathematics or Statistics must be a compulsory subject in graduation.

Program Highlights

  • Globally benchmarked curriculum with supplementary HBR cases
  • Highly industry-relevant electives intricately linked to job requirements
  • Industry projects and internships with an analytics focus

MBA Digital Transformation

A two-year MBA program that focuses on integration of digital technology into business.

Bachelor's degree in Engineering (Any stream) / MCA / B.Sc. (IT) / BSc. (Computer Science) / BSc. (Electronics) / BSc. (Physics) / BSc (Maths) / Any other graduate degree with Mathematics as a compulsory subject. The minimum pass marks of 50% in aggregate (all the subjects for which the candidate has taken & appeared) plus a minimum of two-years of industry experience is desirable.

Program Highlights

  • Integration of management principles with cutting-edge technology
  • Engage in collaborative efforts with universities globally
  • Gain a well-rounded 360-degree learning experience
